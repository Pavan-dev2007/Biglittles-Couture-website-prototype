The Bridal Studio

A step-by-step journey into the creation of your bespoke bridal masterpiece.

01. The Consultation

We begin with an in-depth conversation—either in our Kochi studio or virtually—to understand your vision, preferences, and wedding theme.

02. Design & Sketch

Our head designer translates your ideas into vivid sketches, presenting fabric swatches and hand-embroidery options.

03. The Fittings

Through multiple muslin and toile fittings, we sculpt the silhouette to perfection before the final embellishments are applied.
